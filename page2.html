Past, Present, and the future are 3 stages in our cycle of life

  1. The Past is who we were, what has happened to us, where we have been, and the great things we may or may not have done.
  2. The Present is now, this exact moment when you are searching for motivation online, watching YouTube, procrastinating, or being on-top of our responsibilities.
  3. There is a commont misconception about the future. It could be 5 days, months, or years from today, but the key to the future, is now. Your now, the present of your life, is the deciding factor for your future. Be honest - Are you doing anything in your life that you will benefit from tomorrow, or the day after that? Are you doing something that is holding you back, or even hurting your chances of success in life?
