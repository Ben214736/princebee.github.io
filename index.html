ABOUT BENEDICT

Benedict Mosola, 18 years old, born and raised in Lesotho, Mokhotlong District. He was raised there but he had to leave to live in South Africa, Rustenburg where his mother was working so that he can get a full formal education. He was Matricuated in the year 2017.
Early 2018 he worked at Internet Cafe, he was so comfortable with getting R1000 per month until he was introduced to Network Marketing Company where he realised that he was missing a lot of information. He knew that the decision he took on 14 May 2017 won't affect him immediately but definitely it will in the near future.
